Welcome to My House

A space where engineering meets technology driven by vision, purpose, and innovation.

I am an Engineer, Tech Enthusiast, Leader, Developer, and Founder of Iskify360™. Skilled in Mining & Mineral Processing Engineering and Software Development, with hands-on experience in C, C++, Java, Web Systems, CAD, and 3D Modelling. I craft Android and Web solutions that boost productivity, simplify life, and bring innovative ideas to reality.

My journey is a fusion of engineering precision and digital creativity. From designing real-world systems to building intelligent apps in the digital realm, I thrive on turning challenges into solutions. As the founder of Iskify360™, I lead with vision, innovate with purpose, and create technologies that empower users and communities alike.

At HOBO, we believe that innovation is born at the intersection of precision and creativity. Engineering equips us to shape the physical world, while technology empowers us to transform ideas into impactful solutions. We are committed to crafting systems, apps, and platforms that are not only functional but visionary, bridging the gap between practical engineering and digital innovation. Our vision is to lead in both engineering and technology, creating a future where intelligent software, smart design, and sustainable engineering converge to leave a meaningful impact.
